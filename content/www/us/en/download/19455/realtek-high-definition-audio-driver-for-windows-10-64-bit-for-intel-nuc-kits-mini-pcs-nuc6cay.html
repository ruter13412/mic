Detailed Description

Discontinued Product

This product has been discontinued.  Refer to NUC Discontinuation Notice for more information.

Purpose

This download provides the Realtek* High Definition Audio Driver for the 3.5mm audio jack on the Intel® NUC. This audio driver is required if you plan to connect a microphone or headset to the audio jack.

If connecting to a TV or monitor using an HDMI or DisplayPort cable, the audio is provided through the graphics driver available for your Intel® NUC product.

Not sure if this is the right driver for your Intel® NUC?

Run Intel® Driver & Support Assistant to automatically detect updates.

This download is valid for the product(s) listed below.

  • Intel® NUC Kit NUC6CAYH
  • Intel® NUC Kit NUC6CAYS
